Career Pivots

9 student questions · analyzed by Gemini

Gemini Analysis
What students are really asking: The surface question is "when do you know it's time to leave?" — but underneath, students are wrestling with their own fear of risk. Most are juniors and seniors facing their first major career decision, and they're using Pitaro as a proxy for permission to take the leap. The emotional weight in these questions is unusually high compared to other sessions.

Key pattern: 6 of the 9 questions frame career change in terms of external validation — what others thought, what signs appeared, what made it "safe." Only 2 questions address internal conviction. This suggests the class may benefit from being pushed toward the harder internal question: not "what told you it was okay" but "what would you have done if no one supported you?"
